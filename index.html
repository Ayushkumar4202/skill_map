OBJECTIVE

The core objective of SkillMap is to discover, organize, and apply student talents for fair and efficient participation in school events. By identifying hidden skills that often go unnoticed, SkillMap ensures equitable opportunities for all students, fosters inclusion, boosts confidence, and enhances the quality of school activities. Over time, this system aims to develop students as valuable human resources who contribute meaningfully to society and the nation.
